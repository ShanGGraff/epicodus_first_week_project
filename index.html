Manx Cats: A brief history

According to the Wikipedia page on Manx Cats, Manx cats (earlier spelled Manks) are a bread of cat that originally came from the Isle of Man. They have a mutation that causes them to have short tails or no tails at all. They are prized for there social personalities and were used as ship cats due to their hunting prowess. A Manx that has no tail at all, with only a tuff of hair, is known as a rumpy or dimple rumpy. If the Manx has a bump of cartilage, they are know as a riser or rumpy riser.
